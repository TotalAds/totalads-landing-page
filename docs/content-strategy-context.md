## Content Rules

### Blog Post Frequency
- One solid post every 10 days beats two mediocre posts a week
- Quality and specificity matter more than volume

### Writing Rule
Write every blog post with one specific person in mind:
- NOT "B2B founders"
- YES "a solo founder in India who just set up their first cold email campaign and is confused about why nothing is landing in the inbox"
- The more specific your mental reader, the more useful the post feels to everyone in that situation

### Social Media (LinkedIn Priority)
Platform: Personal founder profile (not company page)
Frequency: 4 posts per week (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri)

Content Buckets:
1. **Behind the scenes (40%)** — Document what you're actually doing
2. **Teach one thing (40%)** — Pick one specific concept, explain it clearly
3. **Opinions and takes (20%)** — Have a point of view, agree/disagree

## SEO Keywords Focus
- Primary: cold email, outbound pipeline, B2B lead generation
- Secondary: AWS SES, email deliverability, domain warmup, email verification
